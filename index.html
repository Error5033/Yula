How we work

A local project like this also provides a better life style and sources to people living around the region. It promotes local crops like cacao, encourages ethical farming practices and guarantees sustainable livelihoods for local farmers and their families. All of the farms supported by our project are Fair Trade.

Our mission is to minimize our negative impact on mother earth. We use 100% recyclable aluminum cans and we’ve worked really hard to source and make sure the ingredients that meet our highest sustainability standards. Our green tea comes from tea gardens certified by the Rainforest Alliance – and off course that our supplier’s farming practices protect the environment, wildlife and the workers.

    There are so many different energy drink companies and there all fell the same, monster, red bull, rock star, there all full of chemicals and still the main difference between them is the flavor, with YULA we aim to improve overall experience of energy drinks, we are using 100% natural irradiance and 100% recyclable aluminum cans.
